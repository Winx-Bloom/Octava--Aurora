Landed Property Terms

C of O
Deed of Assignment
Mortgage

C of O

A document known as Certificate of Occupancy which is vested to an owner having absolute ownership on a landed property

Deed of Assignment

A document that is used to indicate the transfer of ownership of a property to anoth individual duly signed, sealed and delivered with consent from the governor

Mortgage

This involves the collection of loan in a financial institution with the use of someone's property as collateral pending the payment of the loan throughout the loan period
