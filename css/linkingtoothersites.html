Introduction

The Law of Agency is simply the law or legislation that relates to or governs the relationship between a person and his agent(being a person who acts on thr other's behalf

Definition Of Agency

According to Blacks'Law Dictionary,it defines Agency as a 'fiduciary relationship created by express or implied contract or by law, in which one party(the agent) may act on the behalf of another party(the principal) and bind that other party by words or actions'

Key Legal Issues From the Definition Of Agency

The concept of the Definition of Agency must have theses following features

  1. It is a legal relationship in that it is recognised in law
  2. It exists between two people one of which is called the principal and other the agent
  3. the relationship is borne out of an agreement which could be express or implied
